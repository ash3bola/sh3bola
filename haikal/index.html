HAIKAL

Our project to design a visual identity for HAIKAL INVESTMENTS LTD in Cyprus, which operates in diverse fields such as real estate, construction engineering, education, and was designed to fit all the multiple activities of the company. While enhancing professionalism and trust. Thanks to the simplicity of the design, it can be applied to all media and marketing materials, whether digital or printed, without losing its basic identity or clarity. This diversity allows the company to grow and enter new fields easily, without the need for major modifications to its visual identity, which provides continuity and sustainability in the long term.
